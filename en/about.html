About Me

I’m Michael, an IT expert with over ten years of experience, supported by a solid educational background, including my time at the FAAI. My expertise spans programming languages such as C#, PowerShell, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. I specialize in SCCM for server and client software distribution, advanced PowerShell scripting, and designing user-friendly interfaces. I am passionate about creating intuitive, engaging user interfaces and developing efficient automation scripts tailored to my clients' specific needs.

I offer a wide range of services, including advanced PowerShell scripting, SCCM integration, custom website and UI design, and converting PSD (Sketch/Mockup) to HTML/CSS. Additionally, I handle customization and bug fixing. As a fluent speaker of both German and English, I ensure quick response times, professional communication, and top-quality assurance.

Whether you need a simple script modification, an advanced SCCM solution, or a beautifully designed user interface, I provide personalized, high-quality services that are perfectly aligned with your requirements. My dedication to delivering the best possible results makes me a reliable partner for your IT projects.
